Data Strategy:
Developing a data architecture roadmap plan and data governance framework is pertinent to any organization's quest to derive insights and value from data. Aligning data insights and value to an organization's vision and strategic objectives involves designing, defining and implementing a coherent data strategy in order to eliminate data quality issues, technology acquisition prioritization and setting up an effective data analytics center of excellence (CoE) within the organization. At PUFFERFISH Technologies we help you achieve your business objectives with data by designing a best in class data strategy that encompasses technology, people, process and data culture fit within the organization.

Data Governance:
Data Governance framework and implementation are key in deriving the right data culture fit, data ownership incentives, and data steward capabilities in an organization in order to deal with data quality issues, data validation, and data modeling needs. Using the DAMA framework PUFFERFISH delivers the right data governance framework for an organization and ensure key metrics are achieved.

Data Science and Analytics:
Data is the new oil and the need to deliver the right insights, to the right people at the right time is vehemently important in extracting insights from the new oil in order to improve business performance, unlock hidden opportunities for growth and automatically prescribe the right action for a given outcome. With statistical computation and machine learning algorithm PUFFERFISH technologies delivers top-notch data science implementation for today's business needs.
