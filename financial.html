FINANCIAL SOLUTIONS

Work is easy when you have all tools around you!

Cloud computing, often referred to as simply “the cloud,” is the delivery of on-demand computing resources, everything from applications to data centre’s over the internet on a pay-for-use basis.

Moving your businesses to cloud can help you solve real problems by simplifying the process of interlacing applications, infrastructure and services to rapidly bring your ideas to production and scale your business easily. A cloud platform can help your organization drive innovation and business transformation by increasing your business agility, lowering your CAPEX costs, and reducing IT complexity.

At PUFFERFISH, we provide some of the industry’s broadest and most integrated cloud platforms, offering best-in-class services across software as a service (SaaS), platform as a service (PaaS), and infrastructure as a service (IaaS). We also offer flexible and scalable managed services and can help you create and manage their own cloud.
